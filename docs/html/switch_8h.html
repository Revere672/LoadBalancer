Detailed Description

Declaration of the Switch class, the top-level simulation coordinator.

The Switch acts as the entry point for the simulation loop. It owns two LoadBalancer instances — one for primary ('P') requests and one for secondary ('S') requests — and drives the overall clock by calling each balancer's runCycle() every tick.

On a random subset of cycles (~10% probability) the Switch generates a burst of between 1 and 80 new requests and distributes them to the appropriate balancer based on job type.
